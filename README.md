# 🎓 College Complaint Management System (Flask)

## 📘 Project Description
The **College Complaint Management System** is a web-based platform developed using **Flask** that allows students to register their grievances and complaints related to academics, hostel, transport, or facilities.  
Administrators can track, review, and resolve complaints efficiently with real-time status updates and transparency.

## 🧩 Problem Addressed & Proposed Solution

### 🔴 Problem:
- Students face delays and lack of transparency in grievance redressal.  
- Manual complaint systems are inefficient and lack proper tracking or communication.

### 🟢 Proposed Solution:
- A centralized online system for complaint registration and management.  
- Students can track complaint status in real-time.  
- Admins can categorize, update, and close complaints easily.  
- Improves communication between students and administration.
